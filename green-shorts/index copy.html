The Green Shorts Film Festival

The Green Shorts Film Festival is a film festival based around the theme of "GREEN". This could be anything. The color, mother nature, our wonderful city, Kermit the Frog, anything. The sky is the limit, but your film can't be about the sky, because in case you haven't noticed, the sky is blue!
